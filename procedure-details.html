MRI, Lumbar Spine Without Contrast

CPT Code: 72148

About this procedure

An MRI of the lumbar spine produces detailed images of the bones, disks, spinal cord, and the spaces between the vertebral bones where nerves pass through. This non-invasive procedure helps diagnose back pain, spinal stenosis, disk herniation, and other spinal conditions.

Preparation Information
Inform your doctor if you have any metal implants, pacemaker, or other medical devices.
Remove all jewelry, watches, and other metal objects before the procedure.
Wear comfortable clothes without metal fasteners, or change into a hospital gown.
You may be asked to avoid eating or drinking for a few hours before the procedure.
Let your provider know if you have claustrophobia, as you may need a sedative.
